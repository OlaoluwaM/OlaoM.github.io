The “Classical Liberalists” are a team of intellectual IGCSE Global Perspectives students who have carried out various research works to achieve their goal of evaluating health inequality.“Students of Greensprings School Anthony: Ayooluwa, Tise and Dunmomi are IGCSE Global Perspectives candidates who saw a problem in their society and have e valuated their research findings to create a conclusive outcome. These students researched on the effects of health inequality on the less privileged and they have outdone themselves by joining forces to begin a fundraiser and spark a movement among their peers on this issue.” _Doctor Obidahunsi (Interviewee’s review of team project)

Ayooluwa: Working with my team has been a very motivating and uplifting experience. We specially named our team “The Classical Liberalists” because we believe that according to America’s Constitution all men are born same and equal. We are all entitled to the same well-equipped health facilities and should not be denied such due to unequal social status.

Tise: In my view, healthcare is neither a right nor a privilege. A right to health care implies that the force of law can justifiably be used to force health care providers to deliver services regardless of cost and regardless of the opinions and circumstances of those who provide the service. In addition this usually means that taxpayers are also forced to provide the funds to pay for services provided - whether or not they would do so voluntarily or not.

Dunmomi: A privilege implies that a person seeking health care does so by permission, or at least as a less than equal partner in the relationship between patient and care giver. It is reasonable however to state that everyone has the right to SEEK health care from providers and every health care provider has the right to OFFER services. It is also reasonable to state that both sides of the relationship between provider and consumer must enter into the relationship in good faith and conduct themselves in a fair and reasonable way - the same as for any economic transaction.
