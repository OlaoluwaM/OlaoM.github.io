OUR IMPACT

Life’s unpredictable outcomes leave people on the lower end of the scale. Our team’s empathy to this predicament allows us to discover the limitations between the poor in rich in their aim to access good healthcare; we concentrated on children, youth and adults to grasp enough information. Also, finding out what creates restriction in providing appropriate healthcare? We organized a fundraiser to offer free healthcare checkups for “Makoko Slum” inhabitants in our locality.
